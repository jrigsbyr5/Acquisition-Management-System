About This View
Each parcel is colored by its aggregated median of means for price per square foot of land sales. Darker reds indicate higher land values. Use the time period and transaction type filters above to explore how Philadelphia land prices have shifted across different markets and time periods.
Philadelphia Vacant Land
Philadelphia has one of the largest concentrations of vacant land of any major U.S. city — an estimated 40,000+ parcels. These lots reflect decades of population decline, fleeting investment, and industrial abandonment.
Transaction Types
Vacant Land — sales of parcels with no structures, representing raw land value. Market Sales — arm's-length transactions reflecting actual buyer/seller market pricing. Both — combined view showing all qualifying transactions for the period.
